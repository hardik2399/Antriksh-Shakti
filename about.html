About Us

Antriksh Shakti Decor Pvt. Ltd

Antriksh Shakti Decor Pvt. Ltd. is a leading interior and exterior decor solution provider in India, offering a comprehensive range of products including window blinds, wooden flooring, false ceilings, tensile structures, window awnings, outdoor canopies, and wall-to-wall carpet flooring. Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat, we cater to the aesthetic and functional needs of both residential and commercial spaces across India.

As a manufacturer, wholesaler, service provider, and trader, Antriksh Shakti Decor stands out for its commitment to quality craftsmanship, innovative designs, and turnkey decor solutions. Our product range features the latest in architectural decor—including roller blinds, zebra blinds, wooden blinds, skylight blinds, louvered pergolas, and premium-grade floor and ceiling systems—crafted to elevate every space into a design statement.

At Antriksh Shakti Decor, we understand that design is not just about appearance—it’s about creating environments that inspire, comfort, and perform. Our team of seasoned professionals and design consultants work closely with clients, architects, and builders to ensure every product is customized to meet spatial dimensions, color palettes, light dynamics, and functional requirements.

We have successfully delivered projects for top real estate developers, corporate offices, hospitality venues, and retail outlets, earning a reputation for reliability, durability, and innovative execution.

By combining cutting-edge design, high-quality materials, and customer-centric service, Antriksh Shakti Decor Pvt. Ltd. is redefining how you decorate interiors and build outdoor structures.
